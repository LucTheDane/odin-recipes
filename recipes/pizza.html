Pizza

Description

Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 15 mins
Total: 30 mins
Servings: 8
Yield: 8 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza crust dough
  • 1 cup hummus spread
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cups shredded Monteret Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees C (220 degrees C).
  2. Roll out pizza on baking sheet on put on toppings
  3. Bake in oven for 10 to 15 minutes
